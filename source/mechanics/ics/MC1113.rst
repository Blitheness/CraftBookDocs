The '''MC1113''' will teleport a player from a corresponding [[../MC1112/]] IC on a redstone signal.

Sign parameters
===============

#. TELEPORT IN
#. [MC1113]
#. Frequency
#. +oWelcome Text

Pins
====

Input
-----

#. Trigger IC (When ST, disables IC when high)

Output
------

#. High on successful teleport
