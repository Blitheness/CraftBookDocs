=================
Dispenser Recipes
=================

**Dispenser Recipes** are recipes placed inside a dispenser, that perform a task on dispense.

Recipes
=======

Cannon
------

The **Cannon** recipe shoots a lit block of TNT out of the dispenser, with considerable force.
