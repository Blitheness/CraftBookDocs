======
MC4000
======

**MC4000** is an IC that implements a full adder. It adds the two input bits, adds the carry in (third bit), and returns the sum and the carry.


Sign Lines
==========

1. FULL ADDER
2. [MC4000]
3. Blank
4. Blank


IC Pins
=======


Inputs
------

- First Operand
- Second Operand
- Carry Bit

Outputs
-------

- Sum
- Carry Out
- Carry Out (Same as Output 2)
